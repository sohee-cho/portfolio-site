I am... Sohee Cho. A lover of art, especially in the form of graphic design, music, photography, and dance. Currently a student in Communication Design with aspirations to become a ux/ui designer. Originally from Seoul, South Korea and currently based in Brooklyn, NY. Also, a proud cat-mom. Please feel free to contact me at sohcho@gmail.com for questions, comments, or ideas to collaborate!

I went from wanting to be a teacher, maybe, a performer, then to filmmaker until I found the world where art and technology come together. I would like to build websites and apps that have the best user experience and amazing graphic design. I've loved art in every form possible starting at a very young age. Throughout my life, I painted, sang, danced, took photos, edited and filmed videos, and took interest in many "artsy" and creative hobbies. I transferred to City Tech with a goal to grow and become a skilled and talented graphic designer with a focus in Web Design/User Experience. After taking courses Typography 1 and 2, I discovered a newfound interest in typography as well as print design. After graduating, I would like to work with people who desire to make a positive impact in our world and find true joy in it, while being the best at what they do. Some of my influences include: Gustav Klimt, Beyonce, Rifle Paper Co, @nandotattooer, Keith Haring, @mrdanielhan, and Paula Scher
